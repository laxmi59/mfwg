A title tag is the main text that describes an online document. Optimal format: Primary Keyword - Secondary Keyword | Brand Name or Brand Name | Primary Keyword and Secondary Keyword. Search engines will display a maximum of 70 characters in the search results.

Meta keywords are ideal if simply for the sake of organization. Scan the copy on your page, and make a list of the most important terms you see on the page. Pick the terms that most accurately describe the content of your page.

description

The Meta Description provides a clear explanation of your blog post. Make sure that your description is thorough and summarizes your post effectively. Your meta description should be between 150-160 characters.
